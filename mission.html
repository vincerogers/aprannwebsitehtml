Our Mission

Aprann is a faith-based organization whose mission is to promote education, family preservation, and job creation through technical enablement for the underprivileged, and for those who work tirelessly on their behalf.

In order to provide the most effective outreach possible, Aprann has chosen to partner with other nonprofit organizations as the primary vehicle for providing our services. In particular there is a focus on organizations that provide educational opportunities to underprivileged populations, whether that be in the form of early childhood education, primary schools, secondary schools, or adult training and education programs.

There are many wonderful organizations that are already doing incredible work, but often struggle with the overwhelming task of securing the necessary technical resources to reach their maximum effectiveness. We partner with well vetted and proven organizations in order to help them reach full potential and thrive in their unique areas of outreach and expertise.

We strongly believe that by providing software and services in support of educational opportunity and job skills, that we are assisting our partner organizations in their efforts to address systemic and underlying causes of poverty and working to keep families together.
