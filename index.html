    Technology meets mission.

    Equipping nonprofits to thrive in their unique areas of outreach and expertise.

    You're a nonprofit. We're a nonprofit. Our goal is partership, so lets do this together. We partner with strategically aligned organizations in order to help them reach full potential and thrive in their unique areas of outreach and expertise. We strongly believe that by providing software and services in support of educational opportunity and job skills, that we are assisting our partner organizations in their efforts to address systemic and underlying causes of poverty and family disruption.

    • Relational fundraising software
    • Technology assistance through hardware and software consulting, donations, and discounts.
    • Consulting, training, and mentorships.
